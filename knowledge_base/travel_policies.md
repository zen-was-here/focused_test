# Travel Booking Policies

## Booking Terms and Conditions

### Cancellation Policy
- **Flexible bookings**: Free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure
- **Standard bookings**: 50% refund if cancelled 7+ days before, 25% refund 3-7 days before
- **Non-refundable bookings**: No refunds, but can be changed for a fee

### Change Policy
- Changes allowed up to 24 hours before departure
- Change fees: $50 for domestic, $100 for international
- Price differences apply if new booking is more expensive

### Refund Processing
- Refunds processed within 5-10 business days
- Refunds credited to original payment method
- Processing fees may apply for certain booking types

## Travel Insurance

### Coverage Options
- **Basic**: Medical emergencies, trip cancellation ($50)
- **Standard**: Basic + baggage loss, flight delays ($100)
- **Premium**: Standard + adventure activities, pre-existing conditions ($150)

### Insurance Claims
- Claims must be filed within 30 days of incident
- Required documentation: receipts, medical reports, police reports (if applicable)
- Average processing time: 10-15 business days

## Baggage Policies

### Carry-on Allowance
- 1 personal item (purse, laptop bag)
- 1 carry-on bag (max 22x14x9 inches, 40 lbs)
- Free for all passengers

### Checked Baggage
- First bag: $30-50 depending on airline
- Second bag: $40-60
- Overweight/oversized: $100-200 additional fees
- Maximum weight: 50 lbs per bag

## Special Requests

### Seat Selection
- Economy: $15-50 per seat
- Premium Economy: $50-100 per seat
- Business/First: Included

### Special Meals
- Available for dietary restrictions (vegetarian, vegan, kosher, halal, etc.)
- Must be requested 48+ hours before departure
- No additional charge for standard special meals

### Accessibility
- Wheelchair assistance available at no charge
- Must be requested at time of booking
- Airport assistance provided by airlines
