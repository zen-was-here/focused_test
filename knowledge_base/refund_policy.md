# Refund Policy

## General Refund Guidelines

Our company offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products and services. Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase for any reason.

## Refund Process

1. Customer submits refund request through support portal or email
2. Support team reviews request within 24-48 hours
3. If approved, refund is processed within 5-7 business days
4. Refund amount is credited to the original payment method

## Exceptions

- Digital products that have been fully consumed or downloaded are not eligible for refund
- Services that have been completed are not eligible for refund
- Custom orders cannot be refunded after production begins

## Contact

For refund inquiries, contact support@company.com or call 1-800-SUPPORT.
