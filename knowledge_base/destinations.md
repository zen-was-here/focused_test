# Popular Travel Destinations

## Europe

### Paris, France
- Best time to visit: April-June, September-October
- Average flight cost: $800-1200 from US
- Must-see attractions: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame
- Visa requirements: Schengen visa for stays over 90 days
- Currency: Euro (EUR)

### London, UK
- Best time to visit: May-September
- Average flight cost: $600-1000 from US
- Must-see attractions: Big Ben, Tower of London, British Museum
- Visa requirements: No visa for US citizens for stays up to 6 months
- Currency: British Pound (GBP)

### Rome, Italy
- Best time to visit: April-June, September-October
- Average flight cost: $700-1100 from US
- Must-see attractions: Colosseum, Vatican City, Trevi Fountain
- Visa requirements: Schengen visa for stays over 90 days
- Currency: Euro (EUR)

## Asia

### Tokyo, Japan
- Best time to visit: March-May (cherry blossoms), September-November
- Average flight cost: $900-1400 from US
- Must-see attractions: Tokyo Skytree, Senso-ji Temple, Shibuya Crossing
- Visa requirements: No visa for US citizens for stays up to 90 days
- Currency: Japanese Yen (JPY)

### Bangkok, Thailand
- Best time to visit: November-March (dry season)
- Average flight cost: $800-1200 from US
- Must-see attractions: Grand Palace, Wat Pho, Floating Markets
- Visa requirements: 30-day visa-free for US citizens
- Currency: Thai Baht (THB)

## Americas

### New York, USA
- Best time to visit: April-June, September-November
- Average flight cost: Varies by origin
- Must-see attractions: Statue of Liberty, Central Park, Times Square
- Visa requirements: N/A for US citizens
- Currency: US Dollar (USD)

### Cancun, Mexico
- Best time to visit: December-April
- Average flight cost: $300-600 from US
- Must-see attractions: Chichen Itza, Tulum Ruins, Beaches
- Visa requirements: No visa for US citizens for tourist stays
- Currency: Mexican Peso (MXN)
